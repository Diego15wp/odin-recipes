Spanish Flan

Description

Flan is a baked custard topped with caramel. Popular among Hispanics around the world, it is a great dessert that is creamy and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 14 oz of sweetened condensed milk
  • 12 fl oz evaporated milk
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until golden in color.
  3. Pour hot syrup into a 9-inch round glass baking dish, evenly coat bottom and sides
  4. Beat eggs in a large bowl. Add Condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla; beat until smooth
  5. Pour mixture into baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour
  7. flip onto a rimmed serving plate and Cut to serve
