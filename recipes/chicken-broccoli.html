Chicken Broccoli Stir-Fry

Description

This meal is a take out classic. Usually paired with white rice, this "saucy" stir fry will not only kill off your hunger, but leave a delightful sensation on your taste buds. This is a great meal that meets dietary needs and can be made in as little as 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast or Chicken Thighs
  • Broccoli
  • Onions (optional... as well as any other veggies you would like in your Stir-Fry
  • Chicken Broth (can be substituted for water)
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Brown Sugar ~ 2 tbsp
  • Corn Starch - Thickener

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce: Combine the Chicken broth, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Sesame Oil, Ginger, Brown Sugar and Corn Starch
  2. Cook Chicken: Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces; heat a skillet over medium-high heat with 1 Tbsp of oil, put chicken in skillet for 1 min, then striy fry for 5 min, remove Chicken
  3. Sautee Veggies: With same skillet and same heating process, add broccoli and extra veggies and stirfry 3 min then reduce heat
  4. Add Stir Fry Sauce: Add Sauce to skillet Simmer Sauce in low heat for 3-4 min. Add Water to thin sauce and soy sauce to your liking
  5. Mix Chicken Back in Until Heated
  6. Done! Serve Alongside some white rice
